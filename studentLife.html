UiTM

Since the covid-19 pandemic took place during 2019, almost half of my diploma years were online. Semester 1 until semester 3 were fully online while semester 4 was half online. It was challenging for me to study online at home because there were so many commitments that I needed to oblige such as a student, a daughter and a worker. Despite the poor connections and the environment studying online, there was a silver lining for every cloud. During semester 4, it was the first time I studied on campus. I enjoyed staying with my friends in college, doing house chores, staying up with my classmates to do assignments, buy my own meals and wake up early to go to classes. Since my campus was near a paddy field, I could enjoy watching scenery and nature. My final semester was internship thus my stay in Arau, Perlis was just for 2 half months. I have gained good memories until I was there.
